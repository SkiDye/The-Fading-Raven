# BGM Files Needed

Place .ogg files here with the following names:

## Menu
- `menu.ogg` - Main menu background music

## Battle
- `battle_calm.ogg` - Calm battle music (between waves)
- `battle_intense.ogg` - Intense battle music (during waves)
- `boss.ogg` - Boss battle music

## Game End
- `victory.ogg` - Victory music
- `defeat.ogg` - Defeat music
