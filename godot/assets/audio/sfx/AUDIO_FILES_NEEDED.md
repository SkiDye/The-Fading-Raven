# SFX Files Needed

Place .wav or .ogg files here with the following names:

## Hit Effects
- `hit_physical.wav` - Physical damage hit
- `hit_energy.wav` - Energy damage hit
- `hit_explosive.wav` - Explosive damage hit

## Skills
- `skill_shield_bash.wav` - Guardian Shield Bash
- `skill_lance_charge.wav` - Sentinel Lance Charge
- `skill_volley_fire.wav` - Ranger Volley Fire
- `skill_deploy_turret.wav` - Engineer Deploy Turret
- `skill_blink.wav` - Bionic Blink

## Combat
- `enemy_spawn.wav` - Enemy spawning
- `enemy_death.wav` - Enemy death
- `crew_death.wav` - Crew member death

## Facilities
- `facility_damage.wav` - Facility takes damage
- `facility_destroyed.wav` - Facility destroyed

## Waves
- `wave_start.wav` - Wave begins
- `wave_clear.wav` - Wave cleared

## Game End
- `victory.wav` - Victory sound
- `defeat.wav` - Defeat sound

## UI
- `ui_click.wav` - Button click
- `ui_hover.wav` - Button hover
